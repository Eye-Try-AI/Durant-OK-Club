Durant Attractions & Amusements

Choctaw Casino & Resort - Durant
Location: 4216 US Hwy 69/75, Durant, OK 74701
Phone: (580) 920-0160
Hours: Open 24/7
Oklahoma's premier entertainment destination featuring 7,400 slot machines, over 100 table games, 20 restaurants, live entertainment at the Grand Theater, The District (bowling, arcade, movies), two resort pools, luxury spa, and AAA Four-Diamond hotel accommodations.
Fort Washita Historic Site & Museum
Location: 3348 State Road 199, Durant, OK (15 miles northwest)
Phone: Call for information
Hours: Call for current hours
Admission: Free (donations accepted)
Built in 1842 and operated through 1865, Fort Washita protected the Chickasaw and Choctaw Tribes. Today, visitors can explore restored original structures including an officer's quarters and learn about its role in Civil War history. The fort is rumored to be haunted by Aunt Jane's ghost.
Three Valley Museum
Location: 401 W Main St, Durant, OK 74701
Phone: Call for information
Hours: Call for current hours
Named after the three rivers (Red, Blue, and Washita) flowing through the area, this 12,000 square-foot museum showcases the history of southeastern Oklahoma. Features include a Native American Gallery, transportation exhibits with antique vehicles, and a "Small Town Circa 1900-1930" exhibit with historic items from local businesses.
